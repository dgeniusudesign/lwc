DAWN OF JUSTICE

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice is a Superhero movie scheduled for release in 2016 and will be the second of the DC comics shared movie-verse, the first being Man of Steel featuring Henry Cavill as Superman. Henry Cavill will reprise his role as Superman and Ben Affleck will be Batman.

BATMAN

Ben Affleck is Batman and there is no denying that now. Seeing him wear the Batman suit (that can only belong to Christian Bale) has rekindled the hope that Batman can be infact portrayed by someone else other than Bale. Batman is seen wearing a thick armor suit in the teaser.

SUPERMAN

Henry Cavil, with his Superman physique, will continue to reprise the role of the Kryptonian who is on Earth and is trying to save it. Man of Steel showed a dark beginning to the DC shared comic universe, but Henry Cavil got his job done and we all hope that he continues to do so.

CAST

Apart from Batman and Superman, the cast also includes a couple of superheros from Justic League. Jason Momoa will play the role of Aquaman while Mal Madot will be Wonder Woman. Jesse Eisenberg will be playing Superman's nemesis - Lex Luthor.

THE FACE-OFF

In the comics, Batman and Superman are friends, but are involved in a face-off once when the Government sends Superman to take care of Batman(The Dark Knight Returns Animated Movie). This is likely to be scenario in the movie as well.

WHO WILL WIN

Superman is definitely unmatched when it comes to power and energy, but Bruce isn’t a mug either. Bruce is easily the most intelligent and the talented one in the Justice League and if someone can challenge Superman’s powers, it is Batman.
